Laguna State Polytechnic University

Vision

LSPU is a center of technological innovation that promotes interdisciplinary learning, sustainable utilization of resources, collaboration and partnership with the community and stakeholders.

Mission

LSPU, driven by progressive leadership, is a premier institution providing technology-mediated agriculture, fisheries, and other related and emerging disciplines significantly contributing to the growth and development of the region and nation.

STRAND

Information and Communications Technology (ICT) Focuses on computer programming, web development, and network administration, preparing students for careers in the tech industry.

Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS) Emphasizes critical thinking, research skills, and understanding human behavior, ideal for fields like law, journalism, and education.

Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) This strand focuses on developing students' understanding of business principles, financial management, and entrepreneurial skills.

Agri-Fishery Arts (AFA) Offers training in agriculture and fisheries, preparing students for careers in farming, aquaculture, and related fields.
